Kioo

SERVICE: Editorial Design

PROJECT: Personal project

CLIENT: Elisava

YEAR: 2021

BRIEF

The proposed theme is to create a magazine or fanzine that tells the old age linked to man.

The project must be visual and content coherent. It must contain a specific language based on the target to which it is addressed. The project must also be distinguished in terms of content, compared to other magazines on the market.

CONCEPT

Kioo magazine tells the personal stories of men and women that would be lost with the passing of time and old age. They are told through photographs that depict distinct moments of their lives, and that have the goal of intriguing the reader to read their stories.

In addition, the title of the magazine Kioo means mirror in Swahili and wants to represent the mirror inside the camera and the reflection of the stories that tell part of a personâ€™s life.
