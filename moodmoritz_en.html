MoodMoritz

SERVICE: Communication Campaign

PROJECT: Team Project

CLIENT: Politecnico di Torino

YEAR: 2020

BRIEF

The aim of the project is to implement a new communication campaign for Moritz, the Barcelona beer brand.

In the campaign we must communicate through the new media, creating a communication to involve young people from 20 years old, without ignoring the post-pandemic context. In addition, the campaign must emphasize the concepts of modern and classic, authentic and local, which best represent the brand.

CONCEPT

From the union of people, beer and the city of Barcelona, three fundamental and iconic elements for the company Moriz, it's born the communication campaign #Moodmoriz. This is based on the concept that all moods and different personalities are part of Barcelona and are the same locations as the city districts that represent the citizens.

The campaign is based on posters distributed for the city, on # customizable by young people through the website and Instagram account. In addition, six videos have been created in which the #Moodmoritz are told in relation to beer and the different locations of the city.
