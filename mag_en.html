Movimento Attivista Goloso

SERVICE: Communication Campaign

PROJECT: Team project

CLIENT: Politecnico di Torino

YEAR: 2021

BRIEF

The project involves the implementation of a communication campaign related to climate change and activism among young people, with a particular focus on the city of Turin.

It calls for a campaign to highlight a particular consequence of climate change. The aim is to encourage citizens and especially young people to be active in the face of the changes caused by pollution, and to fight for a better future.

CONCEPT

Movimento Attivista Goloso (Mag) is a youth movement that aims to raise awareness among citizens about the direct effects climate change will have on food. In fact, rising temperatures will negatively affect future harvests. Mag therefore wants to raise awareness among young people at a time when they are more sensitive, that is during meals, inviting them to be active.

The three phases, in which the campaign is divided, translate into digital media and just a couple of printed. The two main media are the Instagram account, which has the role of involving and making active the user and the website, a hub that contains all the information related to Mag and activism.
