Olla Festival, St. Caterina Market

SERVICE: Brand Identity

PROJECT: Team project

CLIENT: Elisava

YEAR: 2021

BRIEF

The project involved the rebrand of a commercial activity typical of the city of Barcelona.  The goal was to create a graphic system that was different in each medium but recognizable as the visual identity of the museum. 

CONCEPT

The Santa Caterina Market is a historic and iconic market in the city of Barcelona.  To make it more known and encourage young to go there, it has been designed the Olla Festival in which cooking workshops and aperitifs with seasonal fruits and vegetables are made. The aim of the festival is to introduce the market to the public, making it more accessible to young people and educate them to eat seasonal food.
