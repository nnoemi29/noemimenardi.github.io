Lentezza

SERVICE: Brand Identity and Visual Identity

PROJECT: Personal project

CLIENT: Elisava

YEAR: 2021

BRIEF

The proposed theme is the creation of an editorial catalogue based on the concept of old age linked to man, in one of its many facets.

The project must contain a title and all the basic features of Editorial Design, with a particular attention to typography and layout.

CONCEPT

Old age is a moment in which the frenzy, which characterizes everyday life, begins to fade, replaced by slowness. You begin to observe life from different points of view and, thanks to maturity and slowness, you can understand things that happened before.

The book "Lentezza" tells two stories related to slowness: the story of a elderly woman, suffering from Alzheimerâ€™s, who lives the last years of her life thanks to the support of her son, who tells them through photography. The second story is a fairy tale, which wants to make everyone understand that slowness is not a bad thing but rather allows you to reach new awareness.
